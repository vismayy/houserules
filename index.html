House Rules

This are general rules to be followed by each household members. Change in rules are permitted if majority agrees. Last Update:2025-06-16

  1. In General sense, Household cleaning should be maintained.
    • Each room's cleaning responsibility lies with occupying members.
    • Please follow the cleaning schedule on the same day to ensure maximum hygiene on average all the time.
  2. Please always lock the main door. Every room has given a seperate key in case of lockout.
  3. All the visitors should park their vehicle on the designated spot for the house or in the visitors parking.
