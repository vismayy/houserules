✅ General Rules

These are general rules to be followed by each household members. Change in rules are permitted if majority agrees.

  1. Cleaning: Please follow the cleaning schedule on the same day to maximise the hygiene all the time.
  2. Cleaning: Clean the stove-top after cooking.
  3. Cleaning: After cooking and washing dishes, make sure to empty the drain mesh.
  4. Cleaning: No unwashed dishes should be left in the kitchen for long time unless it is used for storing the food.
  5. Cleaning: Empty the wash bucket after the cleaning.

  6. General: Please always lock the main door. Every room has given a seperate key in case of lockout.
  7. General: For common area, Turn-off lights when not in use.

  8. Washer/ Dryer: Timings to use Washer & Dryer.
    • Weekdays: 07:00 PM to 07:00 AM
    • Weekends: Entire day
  9. Washer/ Dryer: Always remove lint after drying your clothes.
