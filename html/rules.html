These are general rules to be followed by each household members. Change in rules are permitted if majority agrees.

  3. In General sense, Household hygiene should be maintained.
  4. Please follow the cleaning schedule on the same day to ensure maximum hygiene on average all the time.
  5. Each room's cleaning responsibility lies with occupying members.
  6. Timings to use Washer & Dryer.
    • Weekdays: 07:00 PM to 07:00 AM
    • Weekends: Entire day
  7. Do not forget to remove link after drying your clothes.
